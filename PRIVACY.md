# TWL (Today We Learned) — Privacy Policy

**Last updated:** April 2, 2026

## What We Collect

- **Phone numbers** of group participants, provided by the group administrator
- **Message replies** sent in response to daily content drops
- **Engagement data** such as response timestamps and message counts

## How We Use Your Data

- To deliver daily educational content via SMS
- To track group engagement metrics for product improvement
- We do not sell, share, or distribute your personal information to third parties

## Data Storage

- Phone numbers and message data are stored securely in an encrypted database hosted on Amazon Web Services (AWS)
- Twilio is used as our SMS delivery provider and processes phone numbers in accordance with their own privacy policy

## Opting Out

- Reply **STOP** to any message to immediately stop receiving content
- Contact the group administrator to have your data removed

## Contact

For questions about this privacy policy or your data, contact the project maintainer via the [TWL GitHub repository](https://github.com/jtepen/TWL).
